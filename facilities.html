Laboratories

Our well-equipped laboratories provide hands-on learning experiences for science students. Each lab is designed to meet the highest standards of safety and functionality.

Machine Lab

The Machine Lab at Nalanda College of Fashion Design is a fully equipped space designed to provide hands-on training in garment construction and apparel manufacturing. The lab features modern industrial and domestic sewing machines, overlock machines, flatlock machines, and specialized equipment used in the fashion industry.

Computer/CAD Lab

The Computer Lab is an essential part of fashion education at Nalanda College of Fashion Design. It is equipped with high-performance computers, latest fashion design software, and high-speed internet connectivity.

Sewing Lab

The Cutting Lab is specially designed for accurate fabric cutting and garment preparation. It provides students with professional cutting tables, tools, and safety measures essential for fashion production work.

Drafting Lab

The Drafting Lab plays a crucial role in developing students‚Äô skills in pattern making and garment structuring. The lab is equipped with drafting tables, pattern tools, and measurement equipment.

Dyeing and Printing Lab

The Printing Lab at Nalanda College of Fashion Design introduces students to various textile and fashion printing techniques. The lab supports learning in surface design, fabric printing, and creative textile development.
